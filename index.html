About me:

My three children consist of two girls and one boy. I support my kids by working as a Satellite Engineer. I work 4 10-hour days, which gives me three-day weekends to goof off.

I graduated from Arapahoe Comunity College in Spring of 2022 with an Associate of Applied Science degree. For the next two years, I will be studying full-time at CSU Pueblo to get my BS in Computer Information Systems. After that, only one more year to go to get my MS in Computer Information Systems.
